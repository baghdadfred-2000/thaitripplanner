Cookie Policy

Effective date: 21 June 2026 · Applies to thaitripplanner.com

This Cookie Policy explains how thaitripplanner.com uses cookies and similar technologies. We keep this to the bare minimum needed to make the planner work. Our own features rely only on functional local storage; we do not run our own tracking cookies or third-party analytics.

1. What are cookies and local storage?

Cookies are small text files a website can store in your browser. "Local storage" is a related browser technology that stores small amounts of data on your device. Both can be used to remember information between page loads. Our own features rely on local storage for strictly functional purposes; in addition, if advertising is served through Google AdSense it may set third-party cookies, as described in Section 3.

2. What we use, and why

The items below are essential to the Service and are stored on your device only — they are never transmitted to a server we control.

These are classified as strictly necessary because, without them, the planner could not save your progress, keep your theme, or respect your notice preference.

3. Advertising cookies (Google AdSense)

The Service is free and may be supported by advertising served through Google AdSense. To serve, cap and measure ads — and, where you allow it, to personalise them — Google and its partners may set and read cookies and similar identifiers in your browser. These are set by Google, not by us, and we do not have access to the information they collect.

  • Common examples include Google's advertising and frequency-capping cookies (such as those on the doubleclick.net and google.com domains).
  • If you do not consent to personalised advertising, Google may still serve non-personalised ads, which use cookies for frequency capping, aggregated reporting, and fraud/abuse prevention.

You can control or opt out of personalised advertising at myadcenter.google.com, learn how Google uses cookies in advertising at policies.google.com/technologies/ads, and manage vendor choices at aboutads.info/choices or, in the EU, youronlinechoices.eu.

4. What we do not use

  • No first-party analytics or behavioural profiling run by us.
  • No social-media tracking pixels.
  • We do not combine ad data with the trip details you enter — those never leave your device.

5. Third-party content delivery

The Service loads open-source libraries, fonts and map tiles from public networks (Tailwind CSS via CDN, Alpine.js via jsDelivr, Leaflet via unpkg, map tiles from OpenStreetMap, and Google Fonts). Those providers may set their own cookies or process your IP address as part of delivering those files, under their own policies, which we do not control. See our Privacy Policy for more on third parties.

6. Managing your preferences

You are in control of both the functional storage and any advertising cookies:

  • Use the in-page cookie banner or the Cookie Settings page to set your preferences and review what is stored.
  • Opt out of personalised Google ads at myadcenter.google.com.
  • Use the clear buttons on the Cookie Settings page to wipe your saved plan instantly.
  • Clear your browser's site data for this domain, or use a private/incognito window, to remove or avoid stored data.

7. Changes to this policy

If we change how local storage or cookies are used, we will update this page and the effective date above.
