💡 Smart travel tips
  • • Visa: Many nationalities get a 60-day visa exemption — confirm yours before booking flights.

  • • Overland: You preferred to avoid flights — we've kept legs shorter, but a few long-distance transfers are unavoidable over 60 days.
  • • Getting around: Book domestic flights early; use Grab in cities and licensed minivans between towns.
